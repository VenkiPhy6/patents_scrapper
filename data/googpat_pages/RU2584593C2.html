BACKGROUND OF THE INVENTION

1. 1. Field of invention

Corynebacterium sp., , L- .The present invention relates to a microorganism Corynebacterium sp., Modified for the utilization of xylose, and a method for producing L-lysine using such a microorganism.

2. 2. Description of the Related Art

, , . , . , , , , .Industrial microorganisms utilize sugar such as glucose, fructose and sucrose as a carbon source. Agricultural products are commonly used as raw materials for these carbon sources, but they are expensive and more valuable as food. Recently, instead of using agricultural products as traditional raw materials, attention has been drawn to cellulosic biomass, including agricultural or wood processing waste, industrial waste, and so on, as ideal sugar raw materials for fermentation, because it has the advantages of low cost and wide distribution.
