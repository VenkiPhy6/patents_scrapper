[011] A presente inveno auxilia a fornecer uma tecnologia de produo para uma preparao antimicrobiana adequada para uso como aditivo alimentar para animais de pecuria, como por exemplo, aves, bovinos e sunos, que ao mesmo tempo especfico para bactrias oportunistas, como, por exemplo, para cepas patognicas de E. coli que causam incidncia de doena ou morte.[011] The present invention helps to provide a production technology for an antimicrobial preparation suitable for use as a food additive for livestock animals, such as poultry, cattle and pigs, which is at the same time specific for opportunistic bacteria, such as, for example, for pathogenic strains of E. coli that cause disease or death.

[012] A presente inveno auxilia a fornecer uma preparao que poderia substituir os antibiticos atualmente utilizados na pecuria.[012] The present invention helps to provide a preparation that could replace the antibiotics currently used in livestock.

Particularmente, o mtodo compreende ainda a seleo do bacterifago com base em pelo menos um dentre: uma estabilidade entre 4C e a temperatura ambiente (RT); estabilidade em pH 4; e um burst size maior que 10 contra a infeco bacteriana oportunista.[016] In accordance with another aspect of the invention, there is provided a method for selecting a bacteriophage for the prevention of an infection by opportunistic bacteria in animals, comprising: a) isolating a plurality of strains of bacteriophages from a sample environmental; b) characterize the strains of bacteriophages based on the absence of the integrase gene and, optionally, at least one among: variety of bacterial hosts; burst size; toxin gene; antibiotic resistance gene; and 16S RNA gene; c) the selection of at least one of the strains of bacteriophages characterized based on the absence of the integrase gene and, optionally, at least one criterion selected from: absence of the toxin gene; absence of antibiotic resistance gene; and absence of the pathogenic RNA 16S marker gene.
