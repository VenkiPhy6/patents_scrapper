Background technology
Clinically, arthritis is broadly divided into osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, urarthritis, bacillary pass Save inflammation etc., the arthritic symptom of each type and predilection site are all not quite similar, and osteoarthritis is then one of the most common one Kind.Osteoarthritis is a kind of joint disease of degeneration, and the articular cartilage of patient is encroached on, in addition to patient pain is caused, There are the symptoms such as stiff, swelling and deformation, if the lasting progress of this symptom, it is also possible to cause patient's moving difficulty and need crutch or wheel The assistance of chair.
Under general scenario, the osteoarthritis main damage joint that often activity and needs bear a heavy burden, such as knee joint, hip close Section, finger interphalangeal joint, lumbar vertebrae and cervical vertebra etc..The therapeutic modality of osteoarthritis, the originally treatments for adopting Non-surgical more, including： Drug therapy, recovery treating or injection sodium hyaluronate.If can not still slow down the symptom of patient in the above described manner, just using perform the operation as The means for the treatment of.
In drug treatment, steroid medicine can be used, analgesic effect is quick and notable, but its side effect also phase When big, for example, osteoporosis can be generated, wound is not easy to heal, thinning of skin, upper gastrointestinal bleeding etc. or even can aggravate high blood Pressure, the state of an illness of diabetes, therefore, most of situation has all been stopped using at present, but for certain rheumatoid arthritis, red For spot lupus, stiff property spondylitis or Spinal Trauma patient, still need to using steroids alleviate the state of an illness.It may replace steroids The drug of drug has nonsteroid anti-inflammatory drugs and gucosamine.At present, the use of nonsteroid anti-inflammatory drugs is fairly common, analgesic effect Fruit is also all good, but is used for a long time and still has side effect, such as the damage etc. of peptic ulcer, edema of lower extremity, renal function, therefore Reasonable care is also needed using upper.Further, since gucosamine can stimulate intra-articular cartilage cell to synthesize glucoprotein, and have simultaneously The effect that has anti-inflammatory analgetic, the side effect without nonsteroid anti-inflammatory drugs, many patients are also treated using such mode, but due to it The difference of dosage and absorbability, gucosamine have no apparent effect for many patients.
On the other hand, then can be slowed down in intraarticular injection sodium hyaluronate as a kind of lubricant with helping joint motion Symptom.Though sodium hyaluronate can inhibit some inflammatory responses, the effect of slowing down for pain is limited, although making without pair With, but in vivo, as long as a few weeks longer will be absorbed by the body, persistence is not high.
When aforementioned non-operative treatment mode can not all improve patient symptom and mitigate pain, can only carry out with intrusive mood Operative treatment, such as using arthrocsopic surgery, remove the foreign matter in articular cavity or repaired to impaired cartilage, but its for Effect is extremely limited for quite serious patient for joint injury.If directly replacing with joint prosthesis, though it can avoid sending out Scorching, reduction pain, but joint prosthesis also has its service life, if using the simple metal or ceramics of longer life, needs great number Expense.
Therefore, if a kind of drug and its medical composition can be found, be free from side effects, and it is sustainable act, it is special Be not the pain for being capable of reduction of patient, and can by it is most economical, without operative treatment in a manner of reach treatment of arthritis the effect of, It will be the big Gospel of arthritic.
