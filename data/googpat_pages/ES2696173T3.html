2. Descripcin de la tcnica relacionada2. Description of the related art

Las cepas del gnero Corynebacterium, especficamente Corynebacterium glutamicum, son microorganismos grampositivos que se usan ampliamente para producir L-lisina. La L-lisina se usa para piensos para animales, medicinas y cosmticos para seres humanos, y se produce mediante fermentacin de la cepa de Corynebacterium. Strains of the genus Corynebacterium, specifically Corynebacterium glutamicum, are gram-positive organisms that are widely used to produce L-lysine. L-lysine is used for animal feed, medicine and cosmetics for humans, and is produced by fermentation of the Corynebacterium strain .

Convencionalmente, se conoce una cepa del gnero Corynebacterium que tiene genes de biosntesis de lisina potenciados y un mtodo de produccin de L-lisina usando la misma. Por ejemplo, la patente de Estados Unidos n. 6.746.855 divulga un mtodo para producir L-lisina cultivando Corynebacterium que tiene una expresin potenciada del gen lysE (gen del transportador de exportacin de lisina) y adicionalmente genes introducidos seleccionados del grupo que consiste en un gen dapA, un gen lysC, un gen pyc y un gen dapB. Conventionally, a strain of the genus Corynebacterium having enhanced lysine biosynthesis genes and a method of producing L-lysine using the same is known. For example, U.S. Patent No. 6,746,855 discloses a method for producing L-lysine by growing Corynebacterium having enhanced expression of the lysE gene (lysine export transporter gene) and additionally introduced genes selected from the group consisting of in a dapA gene, a lysC gene , a pyc gene and a dapB gene .
