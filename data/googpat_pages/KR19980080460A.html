; . , . , clpPX lon ( 8-140671 ). , .Generation of useful proteins by expression of foreign genes using E. coli can basically be regarded as an established technology; Since many proteins are rapidly degraded in E. coli cells, the level of expression of the desired protein is not always satisfactory. As a means for stably expressing an unstable foreign protein in E. coli, it has been proposed to use a mutant strain carrying a mutation in a protease gene as a host.
