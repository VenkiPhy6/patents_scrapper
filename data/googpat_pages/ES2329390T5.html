El fundamento del ensayo clínico llevado a cabo consiste en que los datos de la bibliografía médica muestran la asociación existente entre la predisposición a las infecciones estacionales similares a la gripe a expensas del sistema respiratorio (ILI y ARI) y la presencia de una inmunidad mucosa insuficiente. The rationale for the clinical trial is that the data in the medical literature show the association between the predisposition to seasonal flu-like infections at the expense of the respiratory system (ILI and ARI) and the presence of insufficient mucous immunity . In particular, what was observed over the years is an increase in the pathologies mentioned above in subjects who, for some reason (such as physical stress, chronic pathologies, aging), had a low protection of mucosal immunity.
