This 1,5-AG has been found to be useful as a control marker for diabetes (see, for example, Non-Patent Documents 1 and 2) As described in Non-Patent Document 1, 1, 5 — AG is a marker between blood glucose level and hemoglobin Ale (HbAlc), and is considered suitable for self-management in patients with mild diabetes.
