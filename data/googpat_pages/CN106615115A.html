Calcium, iron and zinc enhanced yoghurt suitable for student to drink and preparation method for calcium, iron and zinc enhanced yoghurt

Abstract
translated from

The invention discloses calcium, iron and zinc enhanced yoghurt suitable for a student to drink and a preparation method for the calcium, iron and zinc enhanced yoghurt. 100,000 parts of a fresh milk product, 2,000 to 6,000 parts of sucrose, 3 to 4 parts of an iron nutrition enhancer, 2 to 3 parts of a zinc nutrition enhancer, 1,000 parts of old yoghurt fermenting mash, 50 to 70 parts of a calcium nutrition enhancer and 4 to 5 parts of vanillin are adopted. 2 to 6 weight percent of lactobacillus casei WWMR-1 CGMCC (China General Microbiological Culture Collection Center) No.8244 is inoculated, and a stable fermented high-calciate, high-ferrite and high-zincate curd system is established, so that the prepared yoghurt has calcium element content capable of maximally reaching 1,230.5 microgram/ml, iron element content capable of maximally reaching 3.58 microgram/ml and zinc content capable of maximally reaching 4.56 microgram/ml, and good technical effects are achieved.
