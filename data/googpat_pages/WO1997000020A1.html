Enzyme-containing oral composition for animals and process for producing the same

Abstract
translated from

An oral composition for animals which contains thermostable enzymes having a residual activity of 60 % or above after being heated at 80 °C for 20 seconds. It is useful as feed, food or drug that can be taken through the mouths of animals including humans, fish and birds and that are improved in functionality, nutritive value, storability, and in vivo digestibility.
