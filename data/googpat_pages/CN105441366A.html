Application of Bacillus methylotrophicus ZBL-1 in control of cotton verticillium wilt

Abstract
translated from

The invention discloses an application of Bacillus methylotrophicus ZBL-1 in control of cotton verticillium wilt. The Bacillus methylotrophicus ZBL-1 with CGMCC No.11636 and higher inhibition effect on verticillium dahlia is obtained through separation, screening, breeding and domestication of soil samples collected in the cotton fields with severe diseases in Shihezi city and is obviously different from common Bacillus methylotrophicus strains, and meanwhile, the control effect of the screened strain ZBL-1 on the cotton verticillium wilt in indoor pot culture experiments is 73.0%; the control effect of the screened strain ZBL-1 in field plot experiments is 43.1%, and whether in development of a new biopesticide or biological control agent, the Bacillus methylotrophicus ZBL-1 as a biological control material for the cotton verticillium wilt has very good application prospect and has realistic significance and functions.
