Abstract
translated from

The invention relates to a VLA-1 antagonist and uses thereof for treating and preventing an ischemic injury. The VLA-1 antagonist at least partially inhabits the activity or expression of the VLA-1, can induce an antibody or antigen binding fragment of the VLA-1, and can be a peptide, a small molecule and an inhibitor of expression or translation of the VLA-1. The ischemic injury capable of being used in the VLA-1 antagonist treatment comprises an ischemia reperfusion injury, a stroke, a traumatic brain injury and a spinal cord injury. In an implementation scheme of an embodiment, an anti-VLA-1 antibody is a humanized AQC2 antibody, an AJH10 antibody, an hsAQC2 antibody, an mAQC2 antibody, a monoclonal antibody 1B3 or an antigen binding fragment thereof.
