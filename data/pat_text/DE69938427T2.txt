auch zweckmäßig als
synthetisches Intermediat verschiedener Medikamente, einschließlich Methotrexat-Derivaten
(   WO 92/09436   ). Homoglutamic acid is found widely in organisms such as plants, including cholera vibrio as a bacterium and maize (Zea mays), frogs' embryos. Homoglutamic acid serves as an intermediate in lysine biosynthesis in fungi, etc. and as a precursor in the biosynthesis of β-lactam antibiotics. Furthermore, homoglutamic acid is also useful as a synthetic intermediate of various drugs, including methotrexate derivatives (     WO 92/09436     ).
