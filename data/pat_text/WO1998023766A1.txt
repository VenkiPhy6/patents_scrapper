Conventionally, methods for producing ribitol using microorganisms include: (1) a method of producing by enzymatic reaction using D-ribose as a raw material, (2) a method of producing by fermentation of sugar such as glucose, and (3) a method of producing algae. A method using carbon dioxide fixation is known.
