   Mutantes de FAD-2 y plantas con alto contenido de acido oleico Campo de la invencion FAD-2 mutants and plants with high oleic acid content Field of the invention 
 
 
   La presente divulgacion se refiere a plantas, semillas y productos derivados de las mismas, en particular a plantas de Brassica, productos de semillas derivadas de las mismas, que tienen secuencias mutantes que confieren un alto perfil de acido oleico al aceite de semilla. The present disclosure refers to plants, seeds and products derived therefrom, in particular to Brassica plants, seed products derived therefrom, which have mutant sequences that confer a high profile of oleic acid to the seed oil. 
 
 
   Mas particularmente, la presente divulgacion se refiere a secuencias mutantes de acido graso delta-12 desaturasa, tambien denominadas aqul como secuencias de FAD2 mutantes, en tales plantas que confieren un alto perfil de acido oleico sobre el aceite de semilla. More particularly, the present disclosure relates to delta-12 desaturase fatty acid mutant sequences, also referred to herein as mutant FAD2 sequences, in such plants that confer a high oleic acid profile on the seed oil. 
 
 
   Antecedentes Background 
 
 
   La Delta-12 de acido graso desaturasa (tambien conocida como desaturasa oleica u oleato desaturasa) esta involucrada en la conversion enzimatica de acido oleico en acido linoleico. Delta-12 fatty acid desaturase (also known as oleic desaturase or oleate desaturase) is involved in the enzymatic conversion of oleic acid to linoleic acid. 
 
 
   Las variedades con alto nivel de acido oleico (posiblemente combinado con bajo nivel de acido linolenico) se buscan para muchas aplicaciones diferentes (aplicaciones de alimentos, aplicaciones de salud, aplicaciones de biodiesel y muchas otras). Varieties with high level of oleic acid (possibly combined with low level of linolenic acid) are sought for many different applications (food applications, health applications, biodiesel applications and many others). 
 
 
   Las semillas mutantes que proveen un aceite que presentaba un alto contenido de acido oleico (contenido de acido oleico mayor que 70% en peso con base en el peso total de acidos grasos presentes en el aceite) previamente descritas en la literatura tenlan un valor agronomico muy pobre y/o tenlan malas caracterlsticas de ralz, y/o una capacidad de rendimiento muy baja. The mutant seeds that provide an oil that had a high oleic acid content (oleic acid content greater than 70% by weight based on the total weight of fatty acids present in the oil) previously described in the literature had a very agronomic value poor and / or have poor characteristics of ralz, and / or a very low yield capacity. 
 
 
   Todavla existe la necesidad por material que tenga un alto contenido estable de acido oleico (posiblemente combinado con un bajo contenido estable de acido linolenico) a lo largo de los lugares y a lo largo de los anos, con tambien buenos rendimientos agronomicos y con morfologla de semilla oleaginosa de colza normal. En particular, las plantas no deben tener fasciacion y deben tener un desarrollo normal de las ralces. There is still a need for material that has a high stable content of oleic acid (possibly combined with a low stable content of linolenic acid) throughout the places and throughout the years, with good agronomic yields and with seed morphology Oily rapeseed oilseed. In particular, plants should not have fasciation and should have normal root development.

Alternatively, the uptake of nucleic acid molecules can be stimulated by electroporation. In this method, an electrical pulse is used to open temporary pores in a protoplast cell membrane, and said nucleic acid molecules in the surrounding solution are then attracted into the cell through the pores.
