    酵素含有動物経口用組成物およびその製造方法 技術分野   FIELD OF THE INVENTION 
 
 
    本発明は、 酵素を配合した動物経口用組成物およびその製造方法に関 する。 さらに詳しく言えば、 耐熱性を有する酵素を用いることにより、 製造工程での酵素の分解を抑えるとともに保存安定性をも改善した、 酵 素配合飼料、 食料品、 薬剤などの動物経口用組成物およびその製造方法 に関する。 背景技術   The present invention relates to a composition for animal oral administration containing an enzyme and a method for producing the same. More specifically, the use of heat-resistant enzymes suppresses the degradation of enzymes in the manufacturing process and improves storage stability. It relates to the manufacturing method. Background art 
 
 
    酵素は飼料添加物の一つとして用いられている。 例えば、 プロテア一 ゼ、 アミラーゼ、 キシラナーゼ、 セルラーゼ、 グルカナ一ゼ、 ガラク ト シダーゼ等の種々の酵素が、 各種の飼料に添加、 混合されて使用されて いる。 その使用目的の一つは、 家畜が飼料を摂食した際にその消化を促 進することにある。 また、 他の使用目的としては、 飼料作物のサイレー ジ処理時に繊維の分解を促進して良質な乳酸発酵をもたらすことが挙げ られる。   Enzymes have been used as one of feed additives. For example, various enzymes such as protease, amylase, xylanase, cellulase, glucanase, and galactosidase have been added to and mixed with various feeds. One of its uses is to promote the digestion of livestock when feed is consumed. Another use is to promote the decomposition of fiber during silage treatment of feed crops to produce good lactic acid fermentation. 
 
 
    これらの酵素は、 家畜に給餌する時に飼料に混合してもよいが、 酵素 の配合量は飼料に対してごく少量であり、 一般に少量の酵素を飼料中に 均一に混合することは困難である。 また、 微粉体の酵素を飼料中に混合 する場合には酵素微粒子が飛散し給餌者に悪影響を及ぼすおそれがある。 水性溶液として飼料に散布することも考えられるが、 水溶液中では酵 素の分解が進みやすい。 したがって、 飼料製造段階で酵素を添加し、 酵 素を均一に含む配合飼料の形で家畜に与えることが望ましい。   These enzymes may be added to the feed when feeding livestock, but the amount of the enzyme is very small in the feed, and it is generally difficult to mix a small amount of the enzyme uniformly in the feed. . Also, when the enzyme in the fine powder is mixed into the feed, the enzyme fine particles may be scattered and may adversely affect the feeder. Spraying the feed as an aqueous solution is conceivable, but the degradation of the enzyme tends to proceed in the aqueous solution. Therefore, it is desirable to add enzymes to the livestock in the form of a mixed feed containing the enzymes uniformly at the feed production stage.

Production of compound feed  The production process includes high-temperature treatment steps for the purpose of sterilization, drying, etc., and inevitably decomposes and deactivates enzymes. In particular, in recent feed production methods, heated granulators such as pellet mills or extruders have been used to improve moldability and handleability. The degree of enzyme inactivation is relatively small when processing under relatively mild temperature conditions of less than 65 ° C, but there is no problem, but these heating granulators that process at a high temperature of 0 ° C or more Most of the enzymes are broken down in the feed production process. For this reason, there is a need for the development of a technology that allows enzymes to be stably present in compound feeds produced through a high-temperature treatment process.

Having. However, enzymes are generally highly stable in the dry state and have excellent storage stability, but under conditions of high water content, the stability is low and deactivation is likely to occur. In particular, when processing is performed at 70 ° C or more to form pellets, the inactivation of the enzyme is further accelerated. For this reason, it is very difficult to incorporate enzymes into feeds with a high water content to exert their original effects. 
 
 
    また、 酵素配合飼料では常温での保管中にも酵素の失活が進行する。 このため、 通常、 品質維持の観点から製造後短期間に使用する必要があ り、 品質管理の繁雑化や少量単位で輸送することによるコストの増大を 招いている。 このため、 品質管理の簡便化や輸送コストの軽減を図るベ く、 製造直後の酵素活性が長期に亘つて維持できる酵素配合飼料が求め られている。   In addition, in the case of enzyme-containing feed, the enzyme is deactivated during storage at room temperature. For this reason, it is usually necessary to use the product in a short period of time after production from the viewpoint of maintaining quality, which leads to complications in quality control and an increase in costs due to transportation in small quantities. Therefore, in order to simplify quality control and reduce transportation costs, there is a need for an enzyme-containing feed that can maintain enzyme activity for a long time immediately after production.

Another object of the present invention is to provide a composition for oral use in animals which is stable even for long-term storage and a method for producing the same.
