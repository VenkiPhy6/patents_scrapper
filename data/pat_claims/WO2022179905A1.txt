
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
  Claims  
 
 1. A composition comprising at least one of polyphenol and at least one probiotic selected from the group consisting of Lactobacillus paracasei and Lactococcus lactis, for use in treating and/or preventing and/or alleviating respiratory allergy symptoms and/or a respiratory allergy reaction in an individual.  
 
 
 2. The composition for use according to claim 1 , wherein the respiratory allergy is selected from the group consisting of allergic rhinitis, dust mite allergy, or asthma.  
 
 
 3. The composition for use according to claim 1 or 2, wherein the symptoms are selected from the group consisting of ocular symptoms, sneezing, stuffy nose, asthma, fatigue and/or tiredness, anxiety, sleep disorder, mood disorder, depression.  
 
 
 4. The composition for use according to any of the preceding claims, wherein the composition further reduces anxiety, swing/mood disorder and/or depression associated with respiratory allergy.  
 
 
 5. The composition for use according to any of the preceding claims, wherein the composition further supports immune system before and during the pollen season.  
 
 
 6. The composition for use according to any preceding claims, wherein the individual is a toddler.

 10. The composition for use according to any preceding claims, wherein the polyphenol is provided in the form of a plant extract or concentrate, preferably an extract or concentrate from apple, cocoa or tea.
