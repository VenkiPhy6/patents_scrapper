
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 What is claimed is: 
   
 1. A method for making a compound of the structure 
 
 
 
 
   
   
 
 
 
 or a salt thereof, the method comprising:
 treating (1S,3S,5S)-2-(tert-butoxycarbonyl)-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-3-carboxylic acid first with an alkyl chloroformate or mesyl chloride in the presence of a base, and then with ammonia to form (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate; and 
 deprotecting the (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate to form the compound or salt. 
 
 
     
 2. A method according to  claim 1 , further comprising
 saponifying (1S,3S,5S)-2-tert-butyl 3-ethyl 2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,3-dicarboxylate to form the (1S,3S,5S)-2-(tert-butoxycarbonyl)-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-3-carboxylic acid. 
 
 
     
 3. A method according to  claim 2 , further comprising
 treating a mixture of syn and anti isomers of (3S)-2-tert-butyl 3-ethyl 2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,3-dicarboxylate with aqueous methylamine to separate (1S,3S,5S)-2-tert-butyl 3-ethyl 2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,3-dicarboxylate from the mixture; and 
 recovering the (1S,3S,5S)-2-tert-butyl 3-ethyl 2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,3-dicarboxylate. 
 
 
     
 4. A method according to  claim 3 , further comprising
 treating (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate with diethylzinc and chloroiodomethane to form the mixture of syn and anti isomers of (3S)-2-tert-butyl 3-ethyl 2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2,3-dicarboxylate. 
 
 
     
 5. A method according to  claim 4 , further comprising
 esterifying (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylic acid to form ethyl (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylate; 
 reacting the ethyl (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylate with di-tert-butyl dicarbonate to form (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 5-oxopyrrolidine-1,2-dicarboxylate; 
 treating the (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 5-oxopyrrolidine-1,2-dicarboxylate with lithium triethylborohydride, then with base to form the (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate. 
 
 
     
 6. A method for making a compound of the structure 
 
 
 
 
   
   
 
 
 
 or a salt thereof, the method comprising:
 treating (S)-tert-butyl 2-carbamoyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1-carboxylate with diethylzinc and chloroiodomethane to form (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate; then 
 deprotecting the (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate to form the compound or salt. 
 
 
     
 7. A method according to  claim 6 , further comprising
 saponifying (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate to form a 3-carboxylate intermediate; 
 reacting the 3-carboxylate intermediate with methanesulfonyl chloride to form a mixed anhydride intermediate; and 
 reacting the mixed anhydride intermediate with ammonia to form the (S)-tert-butyl 2-carbamoyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1-carboxylate. 
 
 
     
 8. A method according to  claim 7 , further comprising
 esterifying (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylic acid to form ethyl (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylate; 
 reacting the ethyl (S)-5-oxopyrrolidine-2-carboxylate with di-tert-butyl dicarbonate to form (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 5-oxopyrrolidine-1,2-dicarboxylate; 
 treating the (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 5-oxopyrrolidine-1,2-dicarboxylate with lithium triethylborohydride, then with base to form the (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate. 
 
 
     
 9. A method according to  claim 6 , further comprising
 treating an alkali (S)-1-(tert-butoxycarbonyl)-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-2-carboxylate with 4-(4,6-dimethoxy-1,3,5-triazin-2-yl)-4-methylmorpholin-4-ium chloride to form an activated ester; and 
 treating the activated ester with ammonia to form the (S)-tert-butyl 2-carbamoyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1-carboxylate. 
 
 
     
 10. An isolated compound selected from
 (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate; 
 (S)-tert-butyl 2-carbamoyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1-carboxylate; 
 (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate; and 
 (1S,3S,5S)-2-(tert-butoxycarbonyl)-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-3-carboxylic acid. 
 
 
     
 11. An isolated compound according to  claim 10 , that is (S)-1-tert-butyl 2-ethyl 2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1,2-dicarboxylate. 
 
     
 12. An isolated compound according to  claim 10 , that is (S)-tert-butyl 2-carbamoyl-2,3-dihydro-1H-pyrrole-1-carboxylate. 
 
     
 13. An isolated compound according to  claim 10 , that is (1S,3S,5S)-tert-butyl 3-carbamoyl-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-2-carboxylate. 
 
     
 14. An isolated compound according to  claim 10 , that is (1S,3S,5S)-2-(tert-butoxycarbonyl)-2-azabicyclo[3.1.0]hexane-3-carboxylic acid.
