
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 The invention claimed is: 
   
 1. A starter culture for use in preparation of bakery products, comprising homogenously mixed water, flour, and  Lactobacillus brevis  SPC-SNU 70-2 (KCTC 12777BP), wherein the  Lactobacillus brevis  is extracted from Korean traditional Nuruk and exhibiting high acid resistance and maltose availability as well as emission of aldehyde and ketone as fragrance ingredients to flour during fermentation of dough. 
 
     
 2. A bread dough for baking comprising water, flour and  Lactobacillus brevis  SPC-SNU 70-2 (KCTC 12777BP), the dough being obtained by mixing the flour and water, and fermenting the mixture with  Lactobacillus brevis  SPC-SNU 70-2 (KCTC 12777BP) isolated from Korean traditional Nuruk and exhibiting high acid resistance and maltose availability as well as emission of aldehyde and ketone as fragrance ingredients to flour during fermentation.
