METHOD OF MANUFACTURING AN ANTI-MALARIA AGENT characterized by a process for obtaining the compound of 3-chloro-4,6-dimethoxy-2-pentadecylphenol comprising the isolation of said compound from a solution further comprising a cyanobacterium, in particular the strain of cyanobacteria with a deposit under number 1436/1, dated 06/15/2015, at CCAP, comprising the following steps: ✓ Freeze-dry Cyanobium sp cells. LEGE06113; ✓ Extract the compound with a hot mixture, preferably CH2Cl2:MeOH (2:1) at less than 40°C; ✓ Filter the resulting suspension and concentrate in a rotary evaporator.
